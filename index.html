ABOUT US

The Covernotes are your premier live function and events band playing everything from classics to current floor fillers. We bring energy and excitement to your event creating unforgettable experiences that keep your guests on the dance floor.

We are fun and flexible with a professional attitude. We are all experienced in performing live having played hundreds of gigs across numerous countries and venues.

We pride ourselves on being reliable performers, multi-instrumentalists and vocalists providing professional and energetic performances of the highest calibre. We are immaculately turned out, friendly, accommodating and enthusiastic, always giving 100%.

Professional Live Music for Every Occasion

Serving Toronto, Oakville, Mississauga, Burlington, Hamilton, and throughout the Greater Toronto Area, we specialize in weddings, corporate events, private parties, and special functions. Whether you're planning an intimate gathering or a large celebration, The Covernotes delivers exceptional live entertainment tailored to your event.
