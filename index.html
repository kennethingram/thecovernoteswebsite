ABOUT US

The Covernotes is Ontario's premier live function and events band that plays everything from the classics to current floor fillers. We bring energy and excitement to your event, with a diverse repertoire across pop, rock, soul, funk, reggae and contemporary hits to create unforgettable experiences that keep your guests on the dance floor all night long.

We are fun and flexible with a professional attitude. We are all experienced in performing live having played hundreds of gigs across numerous countries and venues.

We pride ourselves on being reliable performers, multi-instrumentalists and vocalists providing professional and energetic performances of the highest calibre. We are immaculately turned out, friendly, accommodating and enthusiastic, always giving 100%. We carry our own professional instruments with a full top of the line PA system.
